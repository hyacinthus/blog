I am a quiet person
Whether in real life or online
I always listen, always read, but rarely speak
Perhaps another way to put it is
I am a lazy person

I rarely ask others questions
As a child, I looked for answers in books
Then the internet came along
And I began asking Google
If I couldn't find an answer, I'd just keep digging on my own
Sometimes a simple problem would consume hours or even days

Still, many questions can be answered online
Right or wrong, I've always been grateful to those who share
That is the spirit of the internet

Yet I feel ashamed
Having stumbled around in the IT industry for so many years
Writing a few lines of personal notes every once in a while
But never anything about technology

So from today on
I'd like to share the small problems I encounter
With everyone here
If by chance just one person sees this
And it saves them a few minutes
I'd be glad
